What are the Effects of Climate Change?

Rising Temperatures

The average global temperature is increasing, leading to more heat waves and extreme heat events. This can affect human health, agriculture, and ecosystems.

Extreme Weather Events

Climate change is making extreme weather events, like typhoons, floods, droughts, and wildfires, more frequent and intense. We're seeing this happen a lot in the Philippines!
